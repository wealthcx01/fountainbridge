## How it runs, briefly

Work becomes tickets in git. Agent lanes on the venture's own box turn each ticket into a branch and a
pull request. The studio reads git and shows you the lanes, the queue of things awaiting your call,
and the health of the whole operation. You approve; git remembers everything.

The full mechanics — gstack, gbrain, ActiveGraph, the ticket flow, the approval matrix — get their
own "how the Foundry works" pages (next), and the method behind building and selling is in the
[playbook](/playbook).
