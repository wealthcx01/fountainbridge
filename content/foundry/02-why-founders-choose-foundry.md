## What you get

- **Velocity without a team.** Lanes work in parallel and around the clock. You review shipped work
  instead of managing people to produce it.
- **Control you can see.** Nothing external — an email, a payment, a deploy — happens without your
  approval, and every one is logged. The studio shows you what's in flight and what needs you.
- **Nothing fails silently.** If a lane stalls or a check breaks, you see *why*, in plain language —
  not a dashboard of green lights hiding a stuck company.
- **A method, not vibes.** Every venture runs on the same disciplined build-and-sell playbook, so
  "what do I do next" always has an answer grounded in evidence.

You stay the founder. Foundry removes the reasons a good founder gets stuck: no team yet, no time, no
operating system.
