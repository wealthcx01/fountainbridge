## Context
FB-013 shipped a summary treatment. John wants this **far more detailed** — a proper working
reference on the two frameworks, applied to Foundry ventures. The frameworks are published
**methods** (ideas, not protected expression), so we can explain and apply them in depth in our own
words, with attribution.

## Scope
- Expand `content/playbook/`:
  - **Disciplined Entrepreneurship (Bill Aulet)** — a section per the 24 steps (grouped by the 6
    questions), each with: what the step means, how to do it for a co-created / interest-based-GTM
    venture, a worked example, and the trap to avoid.
  - **7 Powers (Hamilton Helmer)** — a section per Power (Scale/Network Economies, Counter-
    Positioning, Switching Costs, Branding, Cornered Resource, Process Power), each with Benefit ×
    Barrier, the stage it's buildable, and how a Foundry venture builds toward it.
- **Our own words throughout, with attribution to author + work.** We apply and explain the methods;
  we never reproduce the books' text. Originality is a review gate.
- Cross-linked, behind login (FB-015), grassmarket branding, mobile-usable.
